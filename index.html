Welcome

A calm holiday home by the quieter coast of Tenerife

Casa Abades Tenerife is designed for guests who want a comfortable and peaceful base in South Tenerife. Located in Abades, a small coastal village in the municipality of Arico, the home is ideal for beach days, island exploring, snorkelling, diving and quiet evenings near the sea.

Unlike the busy resort areas of Costa Adeje, Los Cristianos or Playa de las Américas, Abades offers a slower rhythm, local charm and easy access to beautiful coastal scenery.

About Abades

Discover Abades, a peaceful village in Arico

Abades is a quiet coastal village on Tenerife’s south-east coast. It is known for its relaxed atmosphere, beach, local restaurants and easy access to the sea.

It is especially attractive for guests who prefer authentic places, calm surroundings and a good base for exploring the island without staying in the busiest tourist zones.

Quiet coastal atmosphere Good for beach days Great for snorkelling and diving Close to Tajao and Porís de Abona

Things to do near Abades

From beaches and seafood restaurants to volcanic landscapes and day trips, Abades is a practical base for discovering the quieter side of South Tenerife.

Abades Beach

Relax by the sea, swim, sunbathe or enjoy a peaceful walk along the coastline.

Tajao Seafood

Visit nearby Tajao, known for fresh fish and local Canarian seafood restaurants.

Diving & Snorkelling

The waters around Abades are popular with visitors who enjoy marine life and calm sea activities.

Day Trips

Explore El Médano, Montaña Roja, Teide National Park, Costa Adeje and Los Cristianos.
